About me

I started programming in elementary school upon realizing I could create my own games -- my first was a top-down maze on the TI-82 calculator written in TI-BASIC -- and after learning Pascal in high school Computer Science, I knew I wanted a career in software.

Though I didn't wind up making video games, I'm lucky to have had the opportunity to contribute to a wide variety of interesting projects for a diverse set of companies and industries. I'm particularly fond of ASP.NET MVC and maintain a hobbyist's interest in game development and Python, but the primary focus during my ten years as developer has been on C# and the Microsoft .NET web stack, ranging from one-off ASP.NET apps to Internet sites running on SharePoint.
